Who Are We!

Three Girls, CodefirstGirls, Emotional Oranges

After meeting at CodeforGirls, AB, HB, and LN started their adventure on the project Africa Express with the idea of sharing with the world, the often understated beauty of Africa. They call themselves Emotional Oranges. Their goal is to change the narrative of Africa by giving anyone they encounter on their blog, a true image of what Africa really is by sharing true experiences. Hence this interactive and collaborative platform dedicated to tourism and culture on the African continent
